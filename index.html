Locations

Suspended above Titan’s orange clouds, Nebula Core specializes in adaptive resistance training under simulated zero-gravity. The view of Saturn’s rings isn’t just a distraction — it’s motivation.Perfect for endurance athletes and those looking to defy planetary limits.

The Ion Forge turns sweat into power. Industrial architecture meets electric intensity with plasma-lit weight racks and cybernetic trainers. This is where raw strength and machine precision converge — no distractions, just drive.

The Halo District Arena floats in high orbit around Earth, combining panoramic planetary views with AI-guided workouts. Here, gravity is optional, but progress isn’t. For athletes chasing balance between serenity and speed, this is home.
